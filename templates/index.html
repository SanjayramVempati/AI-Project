Inspire

Change the way you learn

What do we do?

Inspire is a software that adminsters a test created by the instructor.
Our AI solution will grade the student answers and format a report at the end of the exam.
Our hope is to address gaps in student learning and dig deeper into a student's test grade.


How it all works

Here is a simple demonstration (no technical stuff)...
1. Teacher inputs an exam with possible correct answers for each question.
2. Our quiz page will administer the test and the student will complete all the questions.
3. Our AI solution will grade the student responses with feedback
4. Our software will create a report for the student and teacher after the exam
